Architecture illustrates the formalization of a desire to learn about the elaborate relation people have between themselves and with the surrounding world.

From concept to reality, an architect goes on a journey of constant creation and reflection, allowing himself to see from a greater perspective than solely his own.

This intense task requires a balanced connection between creativity and logic as the conceptual intentions must be made into something physical with a rational complexity and meaning.

This portfolio is my personal venture through all the challenges, struggles and growth that architecture has promoted during the last five years of my life.

Architectural designs show the designers’ own perception of the world while catering to the needs of the site and the people, so what lies ahead is my own social, cultural and personal constructs, and how they reflect into the designs.

PERCEPTION

Exposition Pavillion in the Eindhoven University of Technology

January 2018

Eindhoven exposition pavillion is designed around the need for connection with its surroudings while mantaining an hierarchy in constrast with them. Hiding the complex interior with an inert but plastic facade, there is created a journey , that gives place to new perceptions. Contrast then is reflected in every scale, from the facade to the tiniest detail, as the user wonders and keeps discovering diferent relations.

To really discover one’s own identity, we have to rearrange perceptions. It is necessary to uncover unkown places filled with doubt and excitement, allowing the mind to be detached from knowledge and perceive everything as new, like the unwarpping of a gift, which drives the mind to imagine and wonder, as there are no rigid nor soft, no curved nor straight, only relations. In the midst of chaos and contrast in these relations, we may find new meanings.

ALEXANDRIA

Designing Sandbox in Eindhoven University of Technology

October 2017

Eindhoven School is formed in the uncertainty of their academic environment, where ideas are developed from individual exploration and precedent study. There is a clear reverance for the modern ideas and expressions, a foundation of rationality with highly expressive highlights.

This project tries to convey a library of the main elements of the great masters of the modernism into a sandbox that allows the creation of buildings through certain conditions that define Eindhoven Architecture.

Through Rhino and Grasshopper, a program is developed that can create different buildings, that are not only able to be optimized, but also allow the selection and printing of different elements into the design: walls, windows, beams and textures.

Acts like an infinite workspace to empower creativity.

CAUSA VANA

Cultural Center and Habitation in Estrela

June 2017

In a city area known for its relevance in the Lisbon urban tissue, the proposal rises as a new perspective over the life of Avenida Infante Santo, Basílica da Estrela and Rua de São Ciro, which manifest as desconnected elements and lack unity.

Through the creation of well defined and regular spaces with unique identities, in a gesture where the mass adapts to the recently shaped voids, there is a search for the promotion of the Basílica and a reformulation of the relation between her, Avenida Infante Santo and the surrounding voids.

Each space reveals a singular essence related to the program of the envolving masses and acts as the conducting wire of the project.

The central courtyard assumes itself as the organizing element of the different moments that define the new theater.

AXIS

Collective Habitation in Belém

June 2016

Belém is a flourishing flower in the new urban tissues of Lisbon, as it is filled with monuments, museums and cultural landmarks. This project starts as a challenge to fit new collective habitation into the barrier between the cultural scale of Mosteiro dos Jerónimos and Centro Cultural de Belém, and the old residential area.

The building is designed where the primary condition is to engage with the surroundings elements by allowing the flow of people, while maintaining a clear but transparent façade between the different scales.

In the interior, there is the desire to create a space that would reflect the same intentions as the city scale. Both typologies have a central elemental which the house revolves around allowing people to wander around their homes while not having an end point, as it is always a journey.

MEMORIES

Cultural Center and Offices in Queluz

June 2015

This project is a reflection over the immense heritage that is present around Queluz, as it used to be the contemplation residence of the old portuguese kings. In a gesture that calls for the past, present and future, a vortex of memories is created where different moments define each part of the design.

In a complex and fluid shape that penetrates the already established aqueduct, this mass tries to connect the city of Queluz, the future, with Palácio de Queluz, the past, reshaping memories in the present.

With the new BIM tools, in this case Revit, the project tests the limits of the mixture of different programs and the relation with old and new, city and forest, lake and sky.

In the end, there is an expectance of a journey of self-knowledge, peace and meditation, unravelling new realizations, the foundation for a new reality.
