Architecture illustratres the formalization of a desire to learn about the intricate relation people have between themselves and with the surrounding world.

From concept to reality, an architect goes on a journey of constant creation and reflection, allowing himself to see from a greater perspective than solely his own.

This intense task requires a balanced connection between creativity and logic as the conceptual intentions must be made into something physical with a rational complexity and meaning.

This portfolio is my personal venture through all the challenges, struggles and growth that architecture has promoted during the last five years of my life.

Architectural designs show the designers’ own perception of the world while catering to the needs of the site and the people, so what lies ahead is my own social, cultural and personal constructs, and how they reflect into the designs.
